The value chain concept outlines the series of activities involved in delivering a product or service from its conception to its final consumption by customers. It encompasses both primary activities, such as inbound logistics, operations, marketing and sales, and service, as well as support activities like procurement, technology development, human resource management, and infrastructure. By analyzing each stage of the value chain, businesses can identify opportunities for efficiency improvements, cost reductions, and competitive advantage, ultimately enhancing their overall value proposition to customers.
